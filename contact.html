Vibrant Ayurveda Wellness Centres, located in Brisbane and the Gold Coast, has been offering authentic ayurvedic services in Queensland for many years.

Headed up by one of Australia’s most experienced and talented ayurvedic practitioners, Vaidya Pradeep Neupane, we offer a team of quality ayurvedic practitioners and massage therapists to meet your every health need.

Gold Coast Centre and Opening Hours

We are delighted to let you know that from Monday 28 June, our Gold Coast Centre will operate from Varsity Lakes. We have secured a convenient and spacious clinic in a shopping strip on Varsity parade. Varsity Lakes is built on beautiful lakes surrounded by luscious green park land. It features a trendy shopping village and beautiful parks at every corner. Character-filled modern homes in tree-lined streets create a lovely setting for our new practice.

Varsity Lakes has Robina Town Centre on one side, Burleigh Heads beach on the other, and has a convenient modern train station. It is only a 5 minute drive to the Gold Coast Highway, 20 minutes to Coolangatta Airport, 20 minutes to Surfers Paradise, and easy access to the M1.

About our centre:

In the new location, we have 4 treatment and consulting rooms, and a spacious reception and waiting area. We are aiming to fill the growing demand for our services in this new vibrant location.
