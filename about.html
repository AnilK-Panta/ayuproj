About Natural Detox (Panchakarma)

Natural Detox at Vibrant Ayurveda Wellness Centre: How To Remove Toxins Using Panchakarma – Ayurvedic Cellular Cleansing Process

Panchakarma is an ayurvedic cellular cleansing process which completely removes toxins from the tissues and eliminates them through the alimentary canal. It is the ultimate mind-body healing experience for detoxifying the body, strengthening the immune system, restoring balance and well-being, and supporting the body’s innate ability to heal itself.

Panchakarma has been used for thousands of years by ayurvedic practitioners in India. Here at Vibrant Ayurveda Wellness Centre, our Ayurveda Practitioner Vaidya Pradeep Neupane have completed specialised panchakarma training in leading ayurvedic wellness centres in Nepal and India. He is proud to offer his knowledge and expertise to the Australian community so you can take advantage of this ancient method for curing illness and promoting health and wellbeing.

Planning Your Natural Detox

Your detoxification program begins with a consultation with one of our experienced ayurvedic practitioners who diagnoses your mind-body constitution, discusses your health concerns, and tailors a program to meet your individual needs.

During the planning consultation, you and the vaidya (ayurveda practitioner) determine the length of your natural detox program, and the number and type of treatments you will have. At the meeting, you are given a detailed booklet that guides you through every step of the process, and provides advice about diet, lifestyle and supplements throughout the program.

1. Prepare the body

To prepare the tissues for mobilising and releasing the toxins, you begin by taking medicated ghee (internal oleation) at home. You also start eating a natural detox diet, and taking prescribed herbs. The ghee, the detox diet, and the herbs work together to draw out toxins from the tissues and build the digestive fire (agni). Without optimal digestion, our food and medicines cannot fully release their nutrients and curative potency.

2. Release and eliminate the impurities

After three to seven days of preparation, you come to the clinic for a series of treatments that support the body to eliminate the toxins that have been drawn out of your tissues. The primary treatments are ayurvedic deep tissue therapeutic massage (abhyanga), sweating in an ayurvedic herbalised steam box (swedana) and herbal enemas (vasti).

Your program can also include herbal purgation to flush the small intestine (virechana), nervous system treatment (shirodhara), nose and sinus treatments (nasya), herbal paste massage (udvartana), and ghee massage. The number and type of treatments you undertake depend on the program your vaidya (ayurvedic practitioner) designs in consultation with you before your detox begins.

Why do a natural detox?

Today, many of us live a fast-paced life which can make it difficult to attend to the fundamentals of ensuring our good health – physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually. Even when we ‘do the right thing’, we live in a world in which it is difficult to avoid environmental toxins. It is inevitable that many of us accumulate toxins to some degree.

If you are suffering symptoms of ill-health, an ayurvedic detox can restore balance to your body, heal your symptoms and bring back feelings of wellness and vitality.

But even if you are not sick, panchakarma is advised — just as you would service your car to keep it running well and to prevent any serious breakdowns in the future.

Health is the first wealth. Your investment in your health and wellbeing will always pay off. Many people feel like they have received a fresh, new body and mind after a panchakarma program at Vibrant Ayurveda Wellness Centre.
