Our People

Mahesh Adikari

Mahesh Adhikari has been working as a Massage Therapist for last 10 years and a most experienced member of our team. Born and grown up in Nepal, his early life taught him the value of natural and holistic living. Before coming to Australia in end of 2012, Mahesh was working as a principal massage therapist and trainer in Nepal’s most popular natural therapy center in Kathmandu-Tranquility Spa. He had trained many people over there.

Training and working in Nepal and Australia, Mahesh has acquired extensive experience in several massage modalities as well as Ayurveda treatments including abhyanga, sirodhara, kati-vasti, nasya, piccu, udwartan and piduchi. In addition, he is very good craniosacral therapist.

He joined the team at Vibrant Ayurveda Wellness Centre when we opened our doors in 2013, and he has worked with many of our clients to bring health, healing and relief to their bodies and minds.

Mahesh’s clients love him! They frequently leave glowing feedback that pays tribute to his soft nature, gifted skills and healing hands. The incredible benefits in pain relief, relaxation and flexibility are the reason his clients keep coming back.

Mahesh’s clients are eligible for rebates from their private health insurance (subject to terms and conditions and type and level of cover).
